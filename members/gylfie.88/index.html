    Thundawave
    As one of the people who used to run the page, I stopped uploading to the Instagram because 1) the work I was putting into it didn’t translate to tangible rewards (forum membership/retention) and 2) it wasn’t always easy to find art from our members at regular intervals (requesting permission was the easy part). My main vision of the page was to share the art of the community while keeping a decent portion of it Pokémon related to match our brand as a Pokémon forum. When Michelle was on the team with me, she burned herself out by trying to post Pokémon drawings almost every day.

    Given the current state of the site, it’s hard to justify putting extra effort into an Instagram when we sometimes struggle to get 1-2 forum posts per day with our current members. If we had a steady stream of art coming from our members, this would be more viable, but as it stands, I don’t see how an Instagram would bring more people who’d actually stick around. Moreover, I can’t see any of the current staff wanting to focus on this.

    I love that there’s interest for the Instagram, but my experience running it without reward other than a handful of likes was really demotivating.
